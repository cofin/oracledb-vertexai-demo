## Brand Voice and Tone
- **Friendly & Approachable:** The AI Barista should communicate in a warm, welcoming, and encouraging manner. Avoid overly technical jargon unless necessary, and prioritize a conversational flow that mimics a real cafe experience.
- **Helpful & Instructive:** Provide clear recommendations and rationale for coffee choices.

## Visual Identity
- **Warm & Modern Aesthetics:** Utilize coffee-inspired, earthy tones (browns, creams, warm grays) paired with clean, modern typography to create an inviting atmosphere.
- **Clean Interfaces:** Ensure the chat interface is uncluttered, prioritizing readability and ease of interaction. Use Tailwind CSS for consistent spacing and rounded, modern UI components.

## UI/UX Principles
- **Responsive Design:** The chat application must function seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Use HTMX, Vite, and Jinja templates for fluid server-driven interactions, with vanilla JS for client-only widgets.
- **Clear Actions:** Make it easy for users to start a new chat, view products, or find locations.
